About

More About Me.

I'm a passionate, and driven person. Eager to learn more and hone my skills. I aim to be able to contribute to the success of a company, and to pursue my goals and gain skills along the way.

I started my journey towards Software development during high school, reading development books recommended to me, and playing around with text editors which sparked my interest and i began to fall in love with the idea of developing software as a career choice.

I consider myself an asset to any team in terms of being easy to work with, a team player, always looking out for everyone's best interest but with a keen and professional eye for what we do in the field. I've gained a strong desire to pursue a career in front end web development where i can not only work with the back end technologies i love but work with people who make that technology truly feel alive and mean something.
